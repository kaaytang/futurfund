Our Story

FuturFund was founded in 2014 by Katherine Tang and Sara Raza, two high school seniors entering university. While they were excited for independence, they also realized that, like many of their peers, they lacked the skills necessary to effectively manage their finances. With a focus on financial literacy skills absent in the school curriculum, they decided to bridge the knowledge gap with FuturFund: a student-run, non-profit organization dedicated to equipping high school students with the skills to make sound financial decisions. Their goal is to provide the next generation with necessary tools and skills in order to make important financial decisions. They do this by connecting youth to inspiring educators and professionals and providing hands on experience through workshops and competitions.
